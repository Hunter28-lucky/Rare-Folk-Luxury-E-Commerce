The Design Story

Carved from history, worn in the present.

The 'Ancestral Lines' motif is not merely a graphic; it is a translation. Inspired by the geometric storytelling found in 18th-century wooden architectural pillars from West Africa, each line and curve represents a specific narrative of community and lineage.

We spent months referencing archival pieces to ensure the weight and placement of the artwork felt intentional. The resulting print is bold yet enigmatic, inviting conversation without shouting for attention. It is a quiet homage to the craftsmen who came before us.
